Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Unstuck ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Unstuck mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect your email address when you register. We collect task text you enter to provide AI-powered step breakdowns. We collect usage data to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and maintain the app, process your subscription, send push notifications, and improve our service. Task text is sent to OpenAI's API to generate step breakdowns and is not used to train AI models.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage, OpenAI for AI features, and Apple for subscription management. Each has their own privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the app, which permanently deletes all your data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at the email below to exercise these rights.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@unstuck.app
